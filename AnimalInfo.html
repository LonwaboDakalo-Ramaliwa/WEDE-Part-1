Pet Related Questions

Why does my puppy / young dog keep biting me?

Your dog is currently teething which is a normal phase every dog goes through to get used to using their teeth properly. if it is over a year old it could be behavourial problems and we suggest monitoring its behevaoiur just to see if the biting is acting out or if it is still in its teething phase.
A guide on teething.

What foods are harmful to dogs?

  • Grapes
  • Chocolate
  • Avocado
  • Chives
  • Yeast Dough
  • Caffeine
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Alcohol
  • Mushrooms
  • Eggs
  • Nuts

We recommend taking your pet/s to a vetinarian to check what other food products can increase health issues in your pet/s.

How long can I leave my dog alone

Depends on the breed because so dogs do not mind the isolation but it is a limited amount so the recommended max is 4 hours and that is only if it is trained and is a 1.5 year old and higher. It can not be left alone for this time period as a puppy it will cause behavourial issues.

What's the best pet for a first-time owner?

Firstly consider your availability with how much free time you have for leisure and have energy to still do activities and your lifetsyle but cats, small dog breeds, hamsters and fish are a good choice for first-time owners.

How often should I feed my cat?

2 times a day if it is an adult but 3 - 4 times if it is still a kitten because it requires a lot of nutrients to develop and grow.
